Zena Hadjivasiliou, PhD

I am an  HFPS fellow working with Marcos Gonzalez-Gaitan at the University of Geneva and Frank Jülicher at the Max Planck Institute for the Physics of Complex Systems. My work is rooted in a curiosity to understand living systems using the beauty and rigor of mathematics and physics. I am currently working on growth control, patterning, and the scaling of morphogen gradients during development. I have worked on a number of different projects and continue to do so.
